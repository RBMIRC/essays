Bernard Stiegler's term: the pedagogical task of reconstructing the knowledges that technique tends to dissolve. If proletarianization is the loss of savoir-vivre (knowing how to live) and savoir-faire (knowing how to do) to machines that absorb and automate human capacities, deproletarianization works against this tendency through technical culture and critical consciousness. Not rejection of technology but appropriation of it—understanding how it works, what it presupposes, how it might be otherwise. (*L'Archive Liquide*)

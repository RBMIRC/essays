Author's term: the criterion of truth in the generative regime. No longer defined by correspondence to a stored record (did this really happen?) but by the capacity of a synthetic artifact to acquire causal traction in the worldâ€”to be believed, shared, acted upon, integrated into networks of reference. The generated image is "true" if it becomes effective, if it durably integrates itself into the fabric of reality regardless of its origin. A pragmatics of plausibility replacing a metaphysics of reference. (*The Recursive Archive*)

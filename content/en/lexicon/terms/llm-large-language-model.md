LLMs are the primary agents of what the corpus terms the transmutation of private code into statistical commons. Their scale (hundreds of billions of parameters) ensures that no individual training instance can be recovered; their capability (generating novel, functional code) ensures that the commons they create is productive rather than merely archival. The LLM is simultaneously the most sophisticated compression algorithm ever created and an inadvertent engine of socialization. (*Heredoc Manifesto*, *The Recursive Archive*)

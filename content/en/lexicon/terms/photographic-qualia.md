Author's term: the perceptual markers that code an image as "photographic" rather than painted or drawn. Includes: depth of field and bokeh (the quality of out-of-focus areas), lens distortion and chromatic aberration, sensor noise with characteristic spatial distributions, motion blur following physical laws. The generator must learn to replicate these signatures to fool the discriminator.

Author's term: the technical manifestation of *retcon*. When a generative model is fed partial archives, it fills gaps by applying statistical continuity, producing "retroactive continuity" where the historical archive presents discontinuity. The model does not retrieve what was but generates what could have been, with no mechanism to distinguish the two. (*Index*)

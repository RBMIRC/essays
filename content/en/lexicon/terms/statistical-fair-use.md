Author's term: proposed doctrine recognizing that probabilistic recombination constitutes sufficient transformation to escape derivative work claims. When less than 1% of outputs correspond verbatim to training data, when patterns from thousands of sources intertwine inextricably, traditional infringement analysis collapses.

Author's term: what the workshop institutes—a site and time where heterogeneous logics can coexist, rub against each other, contaminate one another. Not a closed system with fixed boundaries but a porous space where outside and inside interpenetrate. The workshop recenters on what tends to fade in digital environments (manual gesture, co-presence, material resistance) while welcoming technologies from outside—but bending them to other uses, other rhythms, other purposes than those for which they were designed. (*The Workshop as Form*)

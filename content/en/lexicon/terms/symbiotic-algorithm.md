Luciana Parisi's term: involves parallel processing of binary characteristics that are neither contained within a unique lineage nor filiatively inheritable. Evolution proceeds not by inheritance (parent to offspring, linear descent) but by infection (horizontal transfer, contamination between unrelated lineages).

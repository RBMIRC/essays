Author's term: the transformation of Benjamin's optical unconscious in the era of AI. No longer exploring the hidden structures of the physical world (what the camera reveals about movement, space, matter) but the invisible correlations within data masses (what patterns exist in accumulated visual culture). What AI reveals is not a materiality of the world but a logic of representationâ€”not how things look but how looking has been encoded. The eye no longer looks at the object; it looks at the way culture has looked at the object. (*L'Archive Liquide*)

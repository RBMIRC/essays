The multidimensional mathematical space where machine learning models store their "understanding" of data. Similar concepts are grouped together by proximityâ€”all images of chairs are mathematically close to tables, all portraits close to each other. When we prompt AI, we traverse this space, navigating between points in a high-dimensional archive.

The art of formulating optimal instructions to obtain results from an LLM. The prompt becomes the "new literacy" and the "incantation" addressed to compressed collective intelligence. Where programming required knowing syntax, prompt engineering requires knowing how to speak to statistical distributions. (*The Heredoc Manifesto*)

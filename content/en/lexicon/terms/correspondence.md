Tim Ingold's term for a model of making opposed to the hylomorphic. The practitioner does not project a preexisting form onto passive matter; they follow materials, enter into dialogue with their properties, their resistances, their surprises. The maker corresponds with materials as one corresponds with a friend: through ongoing exchange that transforms both parties. (*The Workshop as Form*)

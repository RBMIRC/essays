Computational architecture composed of layers of interconnected "neurons." It is in the weights of these connections that collective knowledge compresses. The neural network becomes the material substrate where individual contributions dissolve into statistical patterns, making attribution mathematically intractable. (*The Heredoc Manifesto*)

Author's term: the condition under which the statistical commons operates with full material forceâ€”when the information is constitutive of the material instantiation. In domains where this holds, recombination at the informational level produces functional novelty at the material level. Seeds exemplify this: the genetic sequence *is* the organism. Software code exemplifies this: the code *is* the program. Where the constitutive condition holds, the statistical commons has maximal transformative potential. (*Ethics of the Statistical Commons*)

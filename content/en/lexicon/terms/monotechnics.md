The universalization of a single cosmotechnics—the Western one, which aims at mastery of nature through mathematical calculation—imposed on the entire world through colonialism, capitalism, and technological dependency. This forced synchronization crushes local temporalities, alternative cosmologies, other ways of doing technique. Large models (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) are uniformizing infrastructures imposing an average aesthetic, a globalized *vibe*—monotechnics at the level of imagination. (*The Recursive Archive*, *L'Archive Liquide*)

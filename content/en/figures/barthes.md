## Barthes, Roland

**Dates:** 1915-1980  
**Field:** Semiotics, Literary Theory, Photography Theory

### Description

French literary theorist and philosopher whose work on photography in *Camera Lucida* provides foundational concepts for understanding the ontological transformation of the image in the age of AI.

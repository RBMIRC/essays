## Glissant, Édouard

**Dates:** 1928-2011  
**Field:** Philosophy, Literature, Postcolonial Theory

### Description

Martinican philosopher whose concept of opacity—the right not to be understood on the dominant's terms—provides ethical framework for engaging across difference.
